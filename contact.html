Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my Tibet tour?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially if you plan to travel during the peak season (May-October). This allows sufficient time for permit processing and ensures availability of preferred accommodations.

What permits do I need to visit Tibet?

Foreign travelers need several permits to visit Tibet, including the Tibet Travel Permit, Alien's Travel Permit (for areas outside Lhasa), and additional permits for certain regions. As your tour operator, we handle all permit arrangements for you.

Can I travel to Tibet independently?

No, foreign travelers must be part of an organized tour with a licensed guide. Independent travel is not permitted. However, our tours can be private and customized to your preferences.

How do I prepare for the high altitude?

We recommend consulting your doctor before traveling, staying well-hydrated, allowing time for acclimatization, avoiding alcohol, and considering altitude medication. Our itineraries are designed with gradual ascent profiles to help with acclimatization.

What is the best time to visit Tibet?

The best time is from April to October, with May to September being the peak season. Different activities and regions may have specific optimal times, which we can advise on based on your interests.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and WeChat Pay. A deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due 30 days before your tour start date.
