Our Services

Comprehensive travel solutions for your Tibetan adventure

At Beyond Lhasa, we provide end-to-end travel services to make your Tibetan adventure seamless, authentic, and unforgettable. Our team of local experts ensures that every aspect of your journey is carefully planned and executed with attention to detail.

Customized Tour Packages

We design personalized itineraries tailored to your interests, timeframe, and budget. Whether you're interested in cultural exploration, spiritual pilgrimage, trekking adventures, or photography expeditions, we create the perfect journey for you.

Our Tour Packages Include:

  • Cultural Tours - Immerse yourself in Tibet's rich cultural heritage with visits to monasteries, temples, and traditional villages.
  • Spiritual Journeys - Follow in the footsteps of pilgrims on sacred routes like the Mount Kailash Kora.

  • Photography Tours - Capture Tibet's stunning scenery, unique architecture, and vibrant festivals with guidance from photography experts.
  • Festival Tours - Experience Tibet's colorful religious and cultural festivals like Losar (Tibetan New Year) and Saga Dawa.

Tibet Travel Permits

Traveling to Tibet requires several permits. We handle all the paperwork and bureaucracy to ensure a smooth entry into Tibet.

Permits We Arrange:

  • Tibet Travel Permit (TTP) - The basic permit required for all foreign travelers to enter Tibet.
  • Alien's Travel Permit - Required for travel outside Lhasa to places like Shigatse, Gyantse, and Mount Everest.
  • Military Permit - Needed for sensitive border areas like Mount Kailash and western Tibet.
  • Foreign Affairs Permit - Required for specific areas like Tsedang and Samye Monastery.
  • Border Pass - Necessary if you plan to enter or exit Tibet via Nepal.

Note: All permits must be arranged in advance. We typically need 15-20 days to process Tibet Travel Permits.

Experience the unique flavors of Tibetan cuisine and other culinary options throughout your journey.

Our Dining Services:

  • Home-Cooked Experiences - Meals with local families for an authentic taste of Tibetan home cooking.

Altitude Acclimatization Support

Tibet's high elevation (most areas are above 3,500m) requires proper acclimatization. We prioritize your health and safety with our altitude management protocols.

Our Altitude Support Includes:

  • Gradual Ascent Itineraries - Carefully planned schedules that allow proper time for acclimatization.
  • Oxygen Supply - Emergency oxygen available in vehicles and at accommodations when in high-altitude areas.
  • Medical Advice - Pre-trip information about altitude sickness prevention and management.
  • Guide Training - All our guides are trained in recognizing and responding to altitude-related issues.
  • 24/7 Emergency Support - Immediate assistance in case of any health concerns.
